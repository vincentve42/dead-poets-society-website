Dead Poets Society

1989 • DRAMA • 2h 8m

Dead Poets Society is a powerful story set at an elite boarding school where tradition rules and creativity is suppressed. When an unconventional teacher, John Keating, challenges his students to think freely and seize the moment, they discover the courage to question authority, express themselves, and pursue their passions. Through poetry and friendship, the film explores individuality, ambition, and the cost of living authentically.
